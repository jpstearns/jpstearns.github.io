A-Team Tree is the most affordable tree care solution in Rhode Island and operates with a 100% customer satisfaction rating.

OUR TEAM SPECIALIZES IN ALL RESIDENTIAL TREE SERVICES

We believe that Trees are genuinely the most beautiful part of our planet, and just like them we sink our roots deep into Rhode Island soil, and that is why we have dedicated our professional lives to servicing our favorite part of this state, Trees! Our love and respect for nature is what drives our desire to run the most high quality tree service possible. We are fully licensed and insured, with no incidents since we first started doing business in 2013.

COMPANY HISTORY

A-Team Tree was founded in 2013 when two Rhode Island Arbor enthusiasts decided to join teams to deliver the most quality tree service in the State.

OUR MISSION

To do what is best for all trees everywhere, to ensure the safety of our team, and deliver an unforgettable service to our customers.
